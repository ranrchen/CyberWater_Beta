DEMAgent

 Access to DEM data from the SRTM survey.
Downloads DEM data and crops it to the spatial extent.
For more detail on what data is available from SRTM, see https://dwtkns.com/srtm30m/
You need to create an account on Earth Data at the following link: https://urs.earthdata.nasa.gov/
[INPUTS]
case_study: Tuple of TimeRange-SpaceRange defining the spatio-temporal extent of the simulation.
username: Your Earth Data username credentials.
password: Your Earth Data password credentials.
grass_engine GRASS_GISEngine from the GISEngine module.

[OUTPUTS]
dataset_names: Name of the resulting downloaded dataset.
