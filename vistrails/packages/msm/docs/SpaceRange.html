SpaceRange

 module defines the spatial extent of the users simulation.
All Data Agents will download the information available within the limits, at whichever resolution
they have available. The user should be aware of such resolution, and is responsible for changing
it if necessary.

x_min: West-end of the spatial extent.
x_max: East-end of the spatial extent.
y_min: South-end of the spatial extent.
y_max: North-end of the spatial extent.
